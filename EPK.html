The Story

From the Bottom, Up

Rooted in the grit of the 707, Marcus Lohner's latest album, 'Homeless,' serves as a triumphant testament to the rags-to-riches journey. Trading the cold shadows of the Santa Rosa pavement for the warm glow of success, his sound is both a raw reflection of survival and a celebration of the hustle.

Born and raised in Santa Rosa's underrepresented neighborhoods, Marcus Lohner began producing his own beats as a means of survival. Drawing sonic cues from dark, atmospheric alternative R&B and heavy melodic trap, his self-produced project 'Homeless' documents coming straight from the bottom, project to project. Now rackin' up bread and turning heads, his music serves as an anthem for dreamers who remember exactly where they came from.
